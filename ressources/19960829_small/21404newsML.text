Mexican media giant Grupo Televisa said on Thursday it has again extended its offer to exchange a series of private notes for public notes to 5:00 p.m. EDT (2100 GMT) on September 6. As of August 29, about $185 million out of $200 million of series A notes due May 15, 2003 had been exchanged, Televisa said. Some $194 million of $200 million in series B notes due May 15, 2006 had been exchanged also as of August 29, while some $538 million of $570 million in discount debentures due May 15, 2008 had been exchanged, the company said. -- David Luhnow, Mexico City newsroom 525 7289565
