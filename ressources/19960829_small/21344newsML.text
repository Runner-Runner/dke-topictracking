Market leader Unibanka rose slightly on Thursday as volume fell on Latvia's bourse, the Riga Stock Exchange said. Overall market volume was almost 65,000 lats, well down from Thursday's 121,000 lat turnover. Unibanka accounted for just under two-thirds of the total volume, 41,125 lats, and its market price inched up slightly by 0.57 percent to 1.75 lats. --Riga Newsroom +3717-22-66-93
