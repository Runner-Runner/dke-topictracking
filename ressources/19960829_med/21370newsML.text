The Czech National Bank (CNB) fixed the crown at 26.039 to the dollar on Thursday after Wednesday's fixing at 27.168 and at 17.638 to the mark against 17.691. The fixing is announced daily at about 1140 CET (0940 GMT) and is within plus/minus 7.5 percent of a dollar/mark basket midpoint calculated between 11:30:00 and 11:30:59 CET. The above rates imply an appreciation from the midpoint of +4.2 percent, after a deviation of +3.9 percent on Wednesday. -- Prague Newsroom, 42-2-2423-0003
