The Russian Central Bank said it had set 5328/5368 roubles per dollar as its bid and offer rates for its operations on the currency market on Thursday. The official rate for Friday will be 5348. On Wednesday the bid/offer rates were 5328/5368, with the official dollar rate 5348 from Thursday. The Central Bank announces daily bid/offer dollar/rouble rates for its currency operations. The midpoint of these rates is the official dollar/rouble rate for the following day. The daily corridor for the rouble on the interbank market is 5161/5761 after 5158/5758. --Moscow Newsroom, +7095 941 8520
