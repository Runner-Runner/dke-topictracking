Standard & Poor's said on Thursday it will replace Varity Corp in the S&P 500 Index with Dell Computer Corp after the close of trading on September 5, 1996. Varity Corp is merging with the British company Lucas Industries Plc to form LucasVarity Plc, which also will be a British company. The New York Stock Exchange expects trading in Varity to be suspended before the opening of trading on September 6. Dell, a member of the S&P MidCap 400, will be replaced in the index by PMI Group Inc, Standard & Poor's said.
