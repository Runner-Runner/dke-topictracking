First Data Corp said on Thursday that two of its business units will support a pilot program for pre-paid cards recently introduced by Shell Oil Products Co using First Data's PetroCash platform. The pilot program for the Shell Transport and Trading Co Plc /Royal Dutch Petroleum Co unit will give consumers the ability to purchase gasoline and other merchandise at participating Shell stations in two test markets, First Data said. Shell Oil is the largest gasoline retailer in the United States with more than 8,900 Shell stations nationwide.
