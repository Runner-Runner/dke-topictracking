CBOT rice futures closed higher on short covering before Friday, first notice day for deliveries on the September contract, traders said. "I'm expecting zero deliveries for Friday," a trader said. Also traders said U.S. rice export weekly numbers were a little better than expected and the persistent tight stocks scenario continues to keep a floor under the market. Rice futures volume was estimated at 500, up from 147 Wednesday. Rice options volume was estimated at 100, up from 36 Wednesday. Rice futures closed 11 to 14 cents per cwt higher, with September up 14 at $10.36 per cwt. Sam Nelson 312-408-8721
