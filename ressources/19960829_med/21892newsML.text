Atlantic City Electric Co said Thursday its board has authorized a cash tender offer for all outstanding shares of its $7.80 No Par Preferred Stock (cumulative). The Atlantic Energy Co subsidiary said it is offering to purchase all outstanding shares of the $7.80 No Par Preferred at $111.00 per share. The $7.80 series is traded in the over-the-counter market and is not listed on any national securities exchange or quoted on the automated quotation system of a registered securities association, the utility noted. It said the offer and withdrawal rights will expire at 1700 EDT/2100 GMT October 7. The dealer managers for the offer are Goldman, Sachs & Co. and the depositary for the tendered shares will be The Bank of New York. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
