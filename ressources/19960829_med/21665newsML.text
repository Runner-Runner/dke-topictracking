Istanbul gold prices ended slightly lower on Thursday in quiet trade, dealers said. Gold ended 300 lira lower at 1,080,550 lira per gram and $0.25 down at $389.10 per ounce. Volume was slightly higher at 849 kg versus Wednesday's 735 kg. Dealers said trade was quiet ahead of a three-day long weekend. Turkish markets, government offices and banks will be closed on Friday for the national Victory Day. There was 729 kg of trade in the morning and 120 kg in the afternoon. Of the total trade, 63 percent was in lira terms and the rest in dollar terms. -- Istanbul Newsroom, +90-212-275 0875 SA
