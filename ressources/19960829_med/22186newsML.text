Traders said on Thursday that after-hours activity was extremely light, with Sierra Semiconductor Corp trading slightly off its close of 11-7/8. The company traded at 11-5/8, down 1/4, in light volume after the markets closed, traders said. Sierra's stock rose 2-5/8 today after the company said on Wednesday it would sell its computer modem business.   The New York Stock Exchange said its session one volume was 23,900 shares compared with 52,800 shares on Wednesday. There was no session two volume today or Wednesday, the NYSE said. The American Stock Exchange said its after-hours volume was 800 shares compared to no volume on Wednesday.
