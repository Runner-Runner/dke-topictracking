ConAgra Inc said Thursday it has completed the purchase of certain assets of Gilroy Foods from McCormick & Co Inc for about $132 million in cash. A letter of intent for this transaction was announced on March 15, 1996. Gilroy Foods, based in Gilroy, Calif., makes dehydrated garlic and onion products principally for industrial markets. Gilroy Foods' sales in 1995 were about $200 million. Gilroy Foods will report to Raymond DeRiggi, president of United Specialty Food Ingredients Cos, an operating company of ConAgra Trading and Processing Companies. McCormick, based in Sparks, Md., produces spices, seasonings, flavorings and specialty foods. 	     Chicago newsdesk 312 408 8787
