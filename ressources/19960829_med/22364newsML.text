Payless Cashways Inc, a building materials retailer, said its same store sales in August rose 2.5 percent from the comparable month last year. Total sales for the four weeks to August 24, 1996 increased one percent to $214.1 million from $211.9 million a year ago. For the year-to-date, Payless said same store sales fell 3.6 percent from the same period last year. Total sales also declined 3.6 percent to $1.93 billion from $2.00 billion in the 1995 period. Payless operates 200 building materials stores in 22 states. 	     Chicago newsdesk 312 408 8787
