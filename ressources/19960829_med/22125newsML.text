Nordstrom said Thursday co-chairman Raymond Johnson retired. Johnson started with the company in 1969. He managed the downtown Seattle and Bellevue, Wash. stores before he was promoted to vice president and general manager of the company's 12 Washington region stores in 1983. In 1985, he became general manager of the Northern California region, and in 1991 become co- president of the company. In 1995, Johnson was named co-chairman. Nordstrom operations will continue under the leadership of Chairman John Whitacre and Co-Presidents Bill Nordstrom, Blake Nordstrom, Dan Nordstrom, Erik Nordstrom, Jim Nordstrom and Pete Nordstrom.
