Rymer Foods Inc said Thursday the sale of substantially all the assets of Rymer International Seafood Inc was completed. The sale of the business to BGL I Inc was approved August 28 by shareholders. In January, Rymer said it planned to sell the seafood business for about $12.7 million. The company had said it expected to take a $1.5 million net loss on the sale. The final value of the transaction has not yet been determined, a spokesman said, but is still estimated at about $12.7 million. The transaction will incluce cash, a note and assumption of debt.
