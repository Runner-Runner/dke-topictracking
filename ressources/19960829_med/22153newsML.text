Platinum Entertainment Inc said Thursday it has agreed to purchase all rights from Private Music relating to Private Music's 50 percent interest in a joint venture with House of Blues Records for the House of Blues Music Company. House of Blues Records is a subsidiary of House of Blues Entertainment Inc and Private Music is a unit of Private Inc. Platinum, which had limited rights to House of Blues for compilations, now will produce and distribute all current and future artists signed to the House of Blues record label, in addition to unlimited compilations, Platinum said. Platinum also said that during the fourth quarter ended May 31, the company increased its reserves for artists' advances, estimated future returns and other miscellaneous reserves by $3.6 million. As a result, the company reported a net loss before cumulative preferred dividends of $4.8 million compared with a net loss of $4.6 million for the comparable period of the prior fiscal year, the company said. --Reuters Chicago newsdesk, 312-408-8787
