Zydeco Energy Inc said Thursday that it has temporarily abandoned a well being drilled in Bay Marchand, La., and as a result, expects a charge of about $675,000 in the third quarter of 1996.   In a release Thursday, Zydeco said the well was abandoned because of mechanical difficulties and prior to evaluating its primary objectives. Zydeco expects to record a charge of about $675,000 in the third quarter for its share of estimated costs of drilling and abandoning the well. The company said it has a 37.5 percent working interest participation in the well and has incurred $845,000. Zydeco added that it is evaluating alternatives and plans to participate in two separate drillings later this year.
