Chesapeake Energy Corp said on Thursday it expected to report net earnings of $7.4 million, or $0.23 per share, on total revenue of $49.5 million in the fourth quarter ended June 30. This compares with net earnings of $3.8 million, or $0.13 per common share, on total revenue of $23.4 million in the same quarter last year. For the year, it said it expected to report earnings of $23.4 million, or $0.80 per share, on total revenue of $149.4 million, for the fiscal 1996 year ended June 30. Chesapeake expects to release final results for the fourth quarter and year-end periods on or before September 15.   It said that as of June 30, its estimated future net revenue attributable to its proved reserves, before income taxes and discounted at 10 percent, was $547 million, a 193 percent increase over last year's present value of $187 million. Of the company's proved reserves, 45 percent are located in Oklahoma, 41 percent in Texas and 14 percent in Louisiana and other, it said.
