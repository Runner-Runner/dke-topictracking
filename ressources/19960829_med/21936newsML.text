German rolling stock maker Deutsche Waggonbau AG said it had received a major order for 250 double-decker carriages from the German railway group Deutsche Bahn AG. The DWA said the order was valued at about 500 million marks. The carriages will be built at its plant in Goerlitz, preserving 950 jobs, and are for delivery in spring 1997. Deutsche Bahn has bought over 300 such carriages from DWA since 1994. --Frankfurt Newsroom +49 69 756525
