Argentina's Banco del Sud SA said on Thursday that it has issued $25 million in negotiable bonds as part of its $400 million short-term debt program. Bansud said it issued $25 million in bonds due August 20, 1997 at a price of 92.2462. The bond was totally subscribed by Sud Bank & Trust Company Ltd. This is the third issue under the debt program, putting the total sold at $75 million. Bansud directors approved the debt program on May 29. -- Axel Bugge, Buenos Aires Newsroom, 54 1 3180668
