Egypt's Ministry of Supply is tendering to buy 25,000 tonnes of 150-icumsa quality white sugar on September 4, sources in the sugar export trade said. The sugar is for October-November delivery, but payment terms could limit participation to local companies, traders said. The tender is in addition to one by the Egyptian state's Sugar and Integrated Industries Company due on September 17 for 25,000 tonnes of loose raw sugar or packed white sugar, at the buyers option, traders said. --New York Commodities Desk, +1-212-859-1640.
