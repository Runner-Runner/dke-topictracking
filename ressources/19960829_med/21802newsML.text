Yields on the Bond Buyer municipal bond indices rose this week. The yield on the 20-bond index of general obligation bonds calculated Thursday, August 29, was 5.86 percent, up 11 basis points from last Thursday. The yield on the 11-bond index of GO bonds was 5.77 percent, also up 11 basis points from last Thursday.
