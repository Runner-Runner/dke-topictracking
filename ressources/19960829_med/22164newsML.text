Tech Squared Inc said Thursday that Chuck Reese joined the company as president and chief operating officer. Joel Ronning will retain the titles of chairman and chief executive officer. Reese previously was with Weidt Group, where he was vice president of sales and marketing. Tech Squared is a national direct marketer and distributor of high-end microcomputer hardware, software and peripherals to businesses in the desktop publishing, graphic arts and pre-press industries. 	   -- Chicago newsdesk 312 408-8787
