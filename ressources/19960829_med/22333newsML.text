Multi-Market Radio Inc said on Thursday that it bought WMYB (FM) radio station from Puritan Radiocasting Co for $1.1 million. WMYB(FM) which plays 70s rock serves the Myrtle Beach, S.C. market. In April 1996, SFX Broadcasting Inc said that it would acquire Multi-Market Radio. Under the terms, Multi-Market Radio common stock shareholders will receive the stock equivalent of $12.00 per common share. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
