The Tunis Stock Exchange on Thursday closed up by 2.5 points in reduced trade, bourse sources said. The market's 30-share index closed at 617.20 from 614.72 on Wednesday. Trade volume was 433,843 dinars against 23.383 million dinars. Beverages firm SFBT was up 8.52 dinars to 146.39 dinars. ($=0.96 dinar)
