Ming Kown Koo, chairman of Nam Tai Electronics Inc, said Thursday he raised his stake in the company to 42.4 percent or 3,434,361 common shares. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Koo said that between August 13 and 27, he bought 30,000 additional shares of Nam Tai at prices rnaging from $9.375 to $9.625 a share. He said that in addition, Lully Corp, in which he has majority interests, bought 128,000 additional shares between August 13 and 20 for $9.125 to $9.875 a share. Koo said the shares were purchased for investment purposes.
