Indian Bengal-deshi cotton prices on Thursday fell on higher arrivals while other varieties were unchanged in a narrow price band, dealers said. "Bengal-deshi fell on reports of the commencement of new crop arrivals at producing centres," one dealer. In spot deals Bengal-deshi eased by 15 rupees to 860/890 rupees per maund (37.32 kg). Saw-ginned cotton was steady at 1,675/1,700 rupees per maund in spot and October delivery was quoted at 1,650 rupees and November delivery at 1,635 rupees, one broker said. -- Bombay Commodities +91-22-265 9000
