Vicom Ltd said on Thursday it had secured a government tender to install electronic units in Singapore vehicles in conjunction with the implementation of the electronic road pricing system scheduled for end 1997. Vicom, which is a vehicle inspection company, said the Land Transport Authority has appointed it to be the agent for the northern and eastern sectors of the island to install the units. Separately, the company has been appointed by the Land Transport Department of Brunei as the approved agency to export used cars from Singapore to Brunei. - Singapore Newsroom 65-8703080
