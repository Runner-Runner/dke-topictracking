Shop At Home Inc said on Thursday it has entered an agreement for Tele-Communications Inc to bring the home shopping channel into cable subscribers homes. Shop At Home programming will be introduced to no fewer than four million Tele-Communications Inc subscriber households by November 1996 for a minimum of six hours each night, seven nights a week, Shop At Home said. The number of households could grow to as many as 10 million within the first year, the company said.
