Five firms won portions of a $160 million Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority note offering on Thursday. There were 36 bids submitted for the offering. First Albany Corp won $20 million and $32 million of notes; Lehman Brothers won $30 million of notes; Bear Stearns & Co won $20 million of notes; PaineWebber Inc won $50 million of notes; and Prudential Securities won $8 million of notes. --U.S. Municipal Desk, 212-859-1650
