Surging corporate profits and a strong world economy have made U.S. money managers and investors take a closer look at overseas investments, BusinessWeek magazine said in its September 9 issue. In a special report on global investing, BusinessWeek said most of the world's big markets have lagged behind Wall Street's recent rally and now is the time for many of them to catch up. It chose the following top 10 picks for investors :   France's Dassault Systemes, SGS-Thomson Microsystems and Elf Aquitaine, Italy's Stet SpA, Switzerland's Globus, Britain's HSBC Holdings Plc, Bombardier Inc of Canada, Amway Japan Ltd, Hitachi Ltd of Japan and New Zealand's Lion Nathan.
