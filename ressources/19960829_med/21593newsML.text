New World Development Co Ltd was placing 75 million shares at HK$37.10 per share, a source close to the placing agents said on Thursday evening. The shares last traded at HK$38.00, down 90 cents, before being suspended in the afternoon. The shares being placed were held by major shareholders who had agreed to subscribe the same amount of new shares, the sources said. -- Hong Kong Newsroom (852) 2843 6370
