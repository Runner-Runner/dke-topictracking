- Robertson Stephens & Co cut its 1996 earnings estimate for Sierra Semiconductor Corp to $0.80 a share from $1.00 after the company said it planned to exit the modem-chip business, analyst Elias Moosa said. - The brokerage cut its 1997 estimate to $0.65 a share from $1.22, he said. - Still, the brokerage retained its buy recommendation on the expectation the company will emerge smaller but more profitable. - Moosa sees revenues of $190 million this year and $110 million next year, compared with $189 million in 1995. 					-- Seattle bureau 206-386-4848
