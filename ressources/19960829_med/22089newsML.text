Medco Research Inc said on Thursday it authorized the repurchase of an additional 500,000 shares from time to time in the open market. The company said it initiated a 500,000 share stock repurchase plan in September, 1993, and it has so far purchased about 450,000 shares. Now, Medco said it has the authority to buy an additional 500,000 shares for a total of up to one million shares. Medco said it had 10,935,032 outstanding shares as of August 5, 1996.
