Shares of property developer New World Development Co Ltd fell in morning trade on rumours the company is planning a share placement, brokers said. "Talk that the company is planning a share placement triggered fears of possible dilution but the market has no details so far," said a dealing director at a regional brokerage. New World shares fell HK$0.50, or 1.29 percent, to HK$38.40 in late morning trade on volume of 3.12 million shares. New World officials were unavailable for comment. -- Hong Kong Newsroom (852) 2843 6370
