Bastia airport handled 1,065 tonnes of air cargo traffic in the first seven months of 1996, up five percent on the same period last year, airport authority figures show. Air France Europe is the main operator at Bastia, with daily passenger services between Paris-Orly and the northern Corsican city. January-July postal traffic handled at Bastia Airport totalled 3,313 tonnes, an increase of 13.6 percent on 1995 levels. French airline, L'Aeropostale, carries overnight mail between its Paris-Charles de Gaulle hub and Bastia on a regular basis using a Boeing 737QC aircraft.
