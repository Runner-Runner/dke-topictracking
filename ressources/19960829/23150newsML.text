Yukong Ltd, South Korea's largest oil refiner, plans full production at its new 200,000 barrels per day (bpd) crude distillation unit by September 30, company officials said on Thursday.   "We plan to start feeding the crude oil in early September," a Yukong official told Reuters. "Full commercial production is expected by September 30." A company spokesman said Yukong planned to make an official announcement regarding the new crude distillation unit next week. The new unit will raise Yukong's total refining capacity to 810,000 bpd. -- Seoul Newsroom (822) 727 5643
