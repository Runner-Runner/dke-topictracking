German traders bid for 340,000 tonnes of intervention barley and 110,000 tonnes of rye for export to third countries, intervention board BLE said on Thursday. No bids were seen for wheat, the board said.   On the free market, bids were seen for a total 15,000 tonnes of soft wheat, in lots of 3,000 tonnes ranging from taxes of one Ecu to an export restitution of 2.50 Ecus, BLE said. No other bids were seen, it said. -- Bonn newsroom, 49 228 26097150
