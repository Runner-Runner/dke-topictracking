W.W. Grainger Inc said Thursday it won a five-year, multi-million integrated supply contract to supply the aeronautics sector of Lockheed Martin Corp. The agreement consolidates the purchasing of maintenance, repair and operating supplies at several of the sector's largest plants, including sites in Marietta, Ga. and Fort Worth, Texas. Overall, the agreement could consolidate purchasing among several Lockheed Martin sector operations. 	   -- Chicago newsdesk 312 408-8787
