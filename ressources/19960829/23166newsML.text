A subsidiary of Malaysian Resources Corp Bhd (MRCB) has signed an agreement to sell a piece of land to Japan's Daito Trust Construction Co Ltd for 63.4 million ringgit ($25.5 million). MRCB said in a statement on Thursday that the deal is for Daito to develop a four-star hotel in the Kuala Lumpur Sentral project.   Daito plans to build the hotel with gross built-up area of 420,000 square feet, the statement said. MRCB's unit, Kuala Lumpur Sentral Sdn Bhd, is developing the 72-acre site in the Malaysian capital city into a mini-city by 2007. ($1 = 2.49 ringgit)
