Brazil's central bank signalled a new weaker trading level for the real currency by buying dollars at 1.0155 real and selling dollars at 1.0205 real in a buy/sell tender in the commercial foreign exchange market, dealers said. Under the previous mini-band set on August 23, the real was traded between 1.0150 and 1.0200 per dollar. -- Alexandre Caverni, Sao Paulo Newsroom 55-11-2324411
