Indian castor December delivery prices fell to 1,127/1,128 rupees per quintal from 1,130/1,131 on Thursday as sluggish demand and reports of fresh rain in producing areas triggered bull liquidation, dealers said. "Fresh demand from castoroil exporters remained sluggish while reports of fresh rain in the producing centres triggered bull liquidation," one dealer said. Volumes however remained poor. Groundnut oil fell to 425/426 rupees per 10 kg from 428/429 on less physical demand at higher levels coupled with improved arrivals of 70-80 tonnes. In Rajkot spot oil fell to 660/665 rupees per 15 kg from 670/675. -- Bombay Commodities +91-22-265 9000
