Physicians Health Services Inc said on Thursday that Robert Natt will assume the role of president and co-chief executive officer. Michael Herbert will assume the title of co-chief executive officer and third vice chairman for the company. Herbert said he will withdraw from the day-to-day management of the company in which he was the founding president and CEO. Natt, 48, joined PHS in 1985 and served most recently as executive vice president, chief operating officer. In his new role, he will be directly responsible to the board for the management of the company. Physicians Health Services is a full-service health plan serving more than 345,000 members in Connecticut, New York and New Jersey. 	 -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
