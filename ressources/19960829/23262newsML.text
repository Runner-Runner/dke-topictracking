The stock of Malaysian Resources Corp Bhd (MRCB) was suspended on Thursday at the company's request, an official at the Kuala Lumpur Stock Exchange said. Shares of the diversified firm last traded at 7.85, up 25 cents before the suspension. The shares hit a new high of 7.90 ringgit earlier in the session. Officials of the company could not be reached for comment. -- Kuala Lumpur newsroom (603 230 8911)
