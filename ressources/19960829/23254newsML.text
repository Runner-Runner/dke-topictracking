South Korea's Dong Shin Manufacturing Co Ltd said on Thursday it would offer a 24 for 100 rights issue in October for facilities investment. The issue will increase paid-in capital to 13 billion won from 10 billion at present.   A Dong Shin statement said the company plans to issue 600,000 new common shares, of which 20 percent would be offered to its employees. The rest will be allocated to shareholders registered as of September 30. The issue price will be set at a later date. Subscription is scheduled for October 29 and 30, with payment due by November 13. The paper manufacturing company stood at 6,890 won a share in Thursday's afternoon, up 390. -- Seoul Newsroom (822) 727 5644
