The initial offer price for shares in West Japan Railway Co Ltd (JR West) has been set at 357,000 yen per share, the Japanese National Railways (JNR) Settlement Corp said on Thursday. It said the weighted average of bids placed at an auction on Tuesday was 357,377 yen. Shares in JR West, part of the formerly state-owned Japanese Railways group, will be listed on Japanese bourses on October 8. Out of 1.7 million shares JR West is planning to sell initially, 950,000 shares will be sold through the fixed price offer. The rest were auctioned on Tuesday.
