Singapore weekly residue stocks fell by 984,000 barrels in the week ended August 28 to 9.07 million barrels, Singapore's Trade Development Board (TDB) said. They include HSFO, LSFO, LSWR and the straight-run atmospheric residue, but exclude bitumen. Middle-distillate stocks fell by 200,000 barrels to 7.423 million barrels. Middle distillates comprise jet, kerosene and gas oil. Light distillate stocks were down by 168,000 barrels to 4.711 million barrels. They comprise gasoline, reformate and naphtha. -- Singapore newsroom (65-870-3082)
