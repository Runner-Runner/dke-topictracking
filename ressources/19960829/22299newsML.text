HFS Inc, formerly known as Hospitality Franchise Systems Inc, filed Thursday with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer up to $1.0 billion in debt securities and common stock. The company said net proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, which may include acqusitions, repayment of debt, working capital and capital expenditures. The company, which the largest franchisor of hotels and residential real estate brokerage offices, did not name underwriters.
