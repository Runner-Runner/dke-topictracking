Scottish & Newcastle Plc said it named Sir Alistair Grant as non-executive chairman with effect from February 1 next year.   Grant, who has been a non-executive director of the group since 1994, earlier this year announced his decision to retire as chairman of British food retailer and wholesaler Argyll Group Plc. S&N also announced that Brian Stewart would take on the role of deputy chairman in addition to that of chief executive. -- London newsroom +44 171 542 7717
