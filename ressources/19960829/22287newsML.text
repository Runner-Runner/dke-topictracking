Wiz Technology Inc said the resignation of Coopers & Lybrand LLP as its independent auditors "should not affect the timeliness" of its 10K filing, due on or before October 31. In a letter to the Securities and Exchange Commission that Coopers had concurred with company statements in its 8K filing Wednesday, it said there were no disagreements with the former accountant on any matter of accounting principles or practices, financial statement disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure. It also said it interviewing several major auditing firms and expects to engage one of them soon.
