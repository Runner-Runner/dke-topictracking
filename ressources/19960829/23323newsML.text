Shares of Repco Holdings Bhd were up 2.8 percent on requotation after a two-week suspension. At 0150 GMT, the stock was up 1.75 ringgit to 64.25 on volume of 72,000 shares traded. The timber and gaming firm earlier announced its acquisition of a holding company with two sawmill operations for 25 million ringgit. The stock was voluntarily suspended on August 15.
