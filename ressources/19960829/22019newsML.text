The Pakistani rupee closed 0.08 rupees lower at 39.21 to the dollar on Thursday in the kerb market after the State (central) Bank of Pakistan adjusted the rupee down against the dollar, dealers said. They said the rupee was likely to fall further after the Friday weekend on higher demand for the dollar. The central bank moved the rupee lower to 35.6200/35.8337 to the dollar on Thursday from 35.5300/35.7077 fixed on August 13. The premium on foreign exchange bearer certificates closed at 9.90 percent from its previous close at 9.80 percent. -- Ovais Subhani 9221-5685192
