Global cargo traffic handled at Zurich airport in the first seven months of 1996 totalled 253,332 tonnes, a decrease of 1.6 percent on the same period last year, airport authority figures show. January-July 1996 cargo traffic handled by Swiss airlines at Zurich fell by 5.2 percent on 1995 levels to 139,515 tonnes while foreign carriers' volume decreased by 2.3 percent to 44,609 tonnes. Trucked airfreight handled at Zurich in the first seven months of the current year grew by 6.8 percent compared to the same period 1995, to 69,208 tonnes, the airport authority said. The number of all-cargo flights decreased by 76.6 percent on the first seven months of last year to 600 flights.
