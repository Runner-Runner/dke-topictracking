Daimler-Benz AG management chairman Juergen Schrempp said on Thursday that he expects the group's second half profit to be at least as high as profits in the first half. Daimler had earlier reported that group net profit climbed to 782 million marks in the first half of 1996 from a loss of 1.573 billion marks in the first half of last year. --Frankfurt Newsroom +49 69 756525
