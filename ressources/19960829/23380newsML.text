Foreign exchange traders said on Thursday the Bank of France intervened in the market earlier this morning to support the French franc. "The Bank of France sold marks at 3.4295 at around nine o'clock (0700 GMT)," one trader said. By 1120 GMT, the franc was at 3.4267/72 marks after hitting an intraday low of 3.4296 earlier. The Bank of France declined comment on the reports as is its custom in such situations.
