Egyptian pound overnight interbank rates narrowed on Thursday in a very liquid private sector market to between 10 and 10 1/8 percent from 10 and 10 1/4 percent on Wednesday, bankers said. One-week rates on the pound were steady at 10 1/8 and 10 1/4 percent. Public sector banks still maintained their minimum overnight lending rate at 10 1/2 percent. -- Cairo newsroom +202 578 3290/91
