First Liberty Financial Corp said on Thursday its board of directors had authorized a three-for-two stock split of its outstanding common stock to be effected in the form of a 50 percent stock dividend. The record date for the stock split is September 15, 1996, and the additional shares will be issued on or about the payment date of October 1, 1996, the company said. The split will increase the number of shares to six million from four million. Fractional shares resulting from the split will be settled in cash, First Liberty said.
