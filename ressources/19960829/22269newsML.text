Oneida Ltd said on Thursday that it has agreed to buy substantially all the assets and assume certain liabilities of THC Systems Inc, which operates as Rego China, for $45 million. The transaction is expected to be completed by the end of the year, said Oneida Chairman and Chief Executive Officer William D. Matthews. THC is a leading importer and marketer of commercial vitreous china and porcelain for the foodservice industry in the United States, Oneida said.
