First Pacific Co Ltd said its wholly owned Asia Link unit would join China Merchants Holdings Co Ltd to set up a GSM mobile telephone network in Shenzhen, adjacent to Hong Kong. Asia Link will hold 60 percent of the venture, to be known as China Unicom Shenzhen. Total cost of the first phase of the project is about US$29 million. The system aims to have 30,000 subscribers by the end of 1997, the company said. First Pacific said the venture also expected to establish similar GSM services in Dongguan and Huizhou during 1997. China Merchants Holdings is one the controlling shareholders of China Merchants China Direct Investments Ltd. -- Hong Kong Newsroom (842) 2843 6370
