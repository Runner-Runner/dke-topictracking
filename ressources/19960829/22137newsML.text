A group including Anaconda Opportunity Fund L.P., a New York-based investment partnership said Thursday it holds a 5.6 percent stake or 239,659 common shares in Invision Technologies Inc. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it acquired the shares through the coversion of a common stock purchase warrant at an exercise price of $2.2 million or an average price of $9.0702 a share. The group said it holds the stake for investment purposes. Invision is a Foster City, Calif., maker of explosives-detection devices.
