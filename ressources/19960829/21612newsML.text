Sembawang Maritime Ltd (SML) said on Thursday it had agreed with Bangkok-based Montricont and Mobile Co Ltd to set up a container depot at Laem Chabang, Thailand's new deepwater port. It said in a statement the depot was near the port and connected to Bangkok and the rest of Thailand by a good network of highway and rail links. The total investment in the depot by both parties was estimated at Singapore $2.35 million with SML and its associates taking a 60 percent stake, it said. The facility is expected to be operational before the end of 1996. Singapore newsroom 65-8703080
