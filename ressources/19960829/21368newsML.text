Hungary's state privatisation agency APV Rt will announce a new private tender for the sale of Atheneum printing house, APV General Director Istvan Kocsis said on Thursday. "The board declared Atheneum's privatisation tender as unsuccessful on financial grounds and we will announce a new private tender for the sale of the printing house," APV General Director Istvan Kocsis told APV's weekly news conference.  Two bids were submitted for Atheneum's tender, which, unlike the one to be announced, was a public tender for a nominal 1.548 billion forint face value share package or a 90 percent stake in the company's share capital. -- Emese Bartha, Budapest newsroom (36 1) 266 2410
