The Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp (Freddie Mac or FHLMC) said it issued $450 million of Real Estate Mortgage Investment Conduit (REMIC) securities. REMIC series 1889 totals $200 million and is underwritten by DLJ Securities Inc, Freddie Mac said. The issue pays a coupon of 8.0 percent, the agency said. It priced on August 20 and has a settlement date of September 30, Freddie Mac said. REMIC series 1890 totals $250 million and is underwritten by Smith Barney Inc, FHLMC said. The issue has a coupon of 7.5 percent and is backed by 30-year single-family fixed-rate mortgages, the agency said. It priced on August 26 and has a settlement date of September 30, Freddie Mac said. --N.A. Treasury Desk, 212-859-1868
