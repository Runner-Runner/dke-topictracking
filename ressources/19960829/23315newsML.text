Shares in Sunkyong Industry Co Ltd soared in Thursday morning trading on news reports that it would distribute a new hyper-tension medication, brokers said. The shares were trading at 18,100 won, up 500 won after hitting its upper limit of 18,600. Volume was active, with 260,000 shares changing hands. Newspapers reported that Sunkyong would sell the medication, developed by Hankuk Chemical Co. The company was not immediately available to confirm the report. Brokers said the news was making Sunkyoung's stock stand out among a generally dull market. -- Seoul Newsroom (822) 727 5644
