Recoton Corp said it acquired International Jensen Inc after approval of the merger at a Jensen holders meeting Wednesday. Recoton noted the merger occurred after the sale of Jensen's original equipment manufacturing business and said it acquired Jensen's U.S. and foreign consumer electronic branded business activities for home and care stereo loudspeakers, car stereo players and amplifiers for about $55 million, plus the assumption of Jensen's debt of about $30 million. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
