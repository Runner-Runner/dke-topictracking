Shares in British Aerospace jumped to a record high ahead of the Farnborough air show. The show, held just outside London, begins on Monday. In the past, companies have used the Farnborough air show as a forum to deliver good news to the public. By 0848 GMT, the shares were quoted at 1013-1/2, up 17p, after reaching a peak at 1015p. Traders noted several large buy orders, and said the advance above 1000p, seen as a technical barrier, sparked more buying and squeezed shorts out of the market. --Debra Sherman, London newsroom +44 171 542 4026
