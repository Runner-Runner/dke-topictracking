Trading company Hagemeyer NV said on Thursday it was inaugurating the payment of an interim dividend. The company said in a statement with half year results it would pay an interim dividend of 0.75 guilder per share, payable on October 31. Hong Kong's First Pacific Co has a controlling minority stake in Hagemeyer.   "Just checking back through our company records I can confirm that this is the first interim dividend we have paid," a company spokeswoman said. Hagemeyer earlier announced a leap in first half 1996 net profit to 111.3 million guilders from a previous 70.7 million. -- Amsterdam Newsroom +31 20 504 5000
