South Korea's state-run Agricultural & Fishery Marketing Corp (AFMC) said on Thursday it would invite tenders for 20,000 tonnes of U.S. No. 1 soybean for September 5.   An official at the corporation said it will close the bids by 3 p.m. (0600 GMT). The bids will be opened on September 6. He said shipment was set for September 26-October 15 if from the U.S. Gulf or for October 11-30 if from the Pacific Northwest. Arrival was set for the western port city of Inchon by November 20. -- Seoul Newsroom (822) 727 5651
