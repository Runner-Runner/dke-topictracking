Kobe rubber futures were lower in after-hours trade on Thursday, as profit-taking by private investors emerged amid cautious sentiment over recent high prices, traders said. Prices ranged from 0.8 yen to 1.3 yen per kg lower than the day's close. Benchmark February was 1.0 yen down at 142.2 yen. Volume was 8,753 lots. The market started the day on a weaker tone, pressured by technical selling after price gains on Wednesday. Investors were divided over near-term direction. "Direction hinges on overseas markets," an analyst at a commodity broker said.
