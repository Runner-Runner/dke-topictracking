Jamaica is seeking offers to buy 16,500 to 18,000 tonnes of bulk rice under PL-480 terms in three shipments for September through December deliveries, its U.S. agent Fettig and Donalty Inc said. Jamaica seeks three cargoes of 5,500 to 6,000 tonnes of U.S. number 5 or better long grain brown rice for delivery periods September 25/October 15, October 25/November 14, and November 25/December 15. Offers are due no later than 1400 EDT on September 5, 1996, with awards to be made the following day. (Chicago newsdesk 312 408 8720)
