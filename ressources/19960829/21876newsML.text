Shop At Home Inc. said Thursday that Tele-Communications Inc., the nation's largest cable television operator, agreed to make the home shopping channel available in at least 4 million more homes later this year. Shop At Home programmes will be available in the additional homes by November and the number could grow to 10 million in the first year, Knoxville, Tenn.-based Shop At Home said. Shop At Home programmes are now seen in more than 18 million homes daily, it said. Shop At Home also said the Federal Communications Commission has approved its application to buy the 51 percent of KZJL, Channel 61 in Houston, that it does not already own. Terms were not disclosed. The deal is expected to close in early September, Shop At Home said. (Corrects month deal is expected to close in from November).
