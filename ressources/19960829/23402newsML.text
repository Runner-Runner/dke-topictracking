Austrian radiator manufacturer Vogel & Noot Holding AG on Thursday reported a group pre-tax loss of 44.9 million schillings in the first six months after a profit of 33 million in the year-ago period. Sales climbed to 1.82 billion schillings from 1.64 billion, while operating profit slipped to 13.9 million from 102.9 million. The company said it expects full-year group pre-tax profit to reach about 90 to 100 million schillings, down from 131.3 million in 1995. -- Vienna newsroom, +431 53112 274
