Southdown Inc said Thursday it intends to redeem on October 18, on a pro rata basis, 50 percent of its Preferred Stock, $3.75 Convertible Exchangeable Series B that are outstanding on September 9, the record date for the redemption. The redemption price will be $50.00 per share plus accrued dividends to the redemption date of $1.13, for a total price of $51.13 per share, it said. It noted each Series B Preferred share is convertible into 2.5 common shares. Southdown common was trading at 23-5/8 early Thursday. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
