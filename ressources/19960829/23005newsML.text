Rolls-Royce Plc said it had received an order worth up to $450 million to power a batch of Russian-built Tupolev airliners from the Kato Group of Egypt.   The contract with Kato covers RB211-535 engines for 13 firm and 17 option aircraft ordered by the Cairo-based group. Rolls-Royce said the deal represented the production launch of the Rolls-Royce-powered version of the Tupolev Tu-204. -- London newsroom +44 171 542 7717
