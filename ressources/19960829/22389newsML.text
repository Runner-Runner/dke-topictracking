IHOP Corp said on Thursday same-store sales for the fiscal month of August were up 3.7 percent compared with a decrease of 0.9 percent in the same period last year. IHOP develops, franchises and operates International House of Pancakes restaurants. As of June 30, the company had 697 restaurants of which 507 were operated by franchisees, 57 by the company, and 133 by area licensees. -- New York newsroom, (212) 859-1610
