The Bank of England said it is issuing 350 million stg of index-linked Treasury stock on Thursday. The issue comprises 150 million stg of 2.0 percent index-linked Treasury stock due 2006 and 200 million stg of 2.5 percent index-linked stock due 2020. The stock will be priced later and the Bank will receive bids between 0945 and 0950 GMT. -- London Newsroom +44 171 542 5109
