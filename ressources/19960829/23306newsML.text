Philippine National Construction Corp (PNCC) said on Thursday (corrects from Tuesday) it had signed an agreement under which Malaysia's TA Properties Sdn Bhd will build a high-rise mixed-used complex. The complex will be built on a PNCC-owned 23,218 sq metre site along EDSA, Manila's main north-south road, PNCC said in a statement. No further details were available. - Manila newsroom 63 2 8418938 Fax 8176267
