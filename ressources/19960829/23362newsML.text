The average yield at this week's tender for 26.779 million dinars of six-month Kuwaiti treasury bills rose to 7.240 percent from 7.218 percent last week, official figures show. The bills, offered on Monday, were issued on Wednesday. They mature on February 19. The average accepted price was 96.645. Bids were made for 26.779 million dinars of the six-month bills. The highest accepted price was 96.641, with a yield of 7.249 percent. The lowest accepted price was 96.696, with a yield of 7.127 percent. There was no tender this week for three-month bills. -- Kuwait Newsroom, +965 240 8945, fax 241 2459
