The former Interdiscount Holding AG, now named Distefora, said it completed the previously announced sale of 100 percent of its French photo retail unit Interdiscount France S.A. to Belgium photo processing group Spector NV. Distefora also said in a statement that the planned sale of its 58.1 percent stake in Porst Holding AG to Spector will be completed in the coming weeks. Details on the transactions will be provided at a news conference to be set for September 6, the statement also said. -- Zurich Editorial +41 1 631 7340
