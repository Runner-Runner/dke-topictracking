LecTec Corp said Wednesday it and  Medtek Asia Private Limited executed an agreement under which LecTec will manufacture and sell medical tape master rolls to Medtek Asia. The master rolls of medical tape will be converted by Medtek Asia into finished medical tape products which will be marketed into the Indian healthcare system under the trade names Dermapore, Dermasilk and Dermasynth. 	  -- Chicago newsdesk 312 408-8787
