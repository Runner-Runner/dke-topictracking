Hungary's Budapest Interbank Offered Rate (BUBOR) shed 0.03 percentage point for the three-month maturity but remained steady for the one month, the National Bank of Hungary (NBH) said on Thursday. The BUBOR was fixed by the NBH at 23.50 percent for both the one-month and the three-month maturity. The NBH began calculating BUBOR on August 1. -- Budapest newsroom, +36 1 266 2410
