Medical Technology and Innovations Inc said Thursday its board has approved the buy-back of 100,000 shares in the open market over the next 12 months. Chief Financial Officer Steve Gill said, "It is managements' opinion that the stock is severely undervalued." The company said directors also approved a resolution to buy back all shares from shareholders who own less than 100 shares. This represents about 11,000 shares from 480 shareholders. Medical Technology now has 11.5 million shares outstanding. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
