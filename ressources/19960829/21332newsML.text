Poland's Securities Commission (KPW) on Thursday cleared poultry producer  Drosed Siedleckie Zaklady Drobiarskie SA (Drosed) new issue of 160,000 to 300,000 shares for public trading, the company said in a statement. The company earlier said the offering would be divided into two tranches, for large and smaller investors. -- Warsaw Newsroom +48 22 653 9700
