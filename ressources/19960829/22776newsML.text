Shares in Finnish state-controlled chemicals group Kemira Oy continued to slide in lively trade on Thursday afternoon depressed by a profit warning released on Tuesday, brokers said. At 1520/1220 GMT, Kemira traded at a two-week low 47.50, down 1.50 markka or some three percent. Trade was a lively 14 million markka. There were mainly foreign investors selling Kemira, brokers said. "Some big foreign investors have drawn their conclusions from the profit warning," one broker said. -- Helsinki newsroom +358-0-6805 0248
