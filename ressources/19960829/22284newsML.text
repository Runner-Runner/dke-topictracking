C.H. Heist Corp said it expects to complete the spin-off and initial public offering of Ablest Service Corp, its temporary staffing unit, by the end of the current fiscal year. C.H. Heist said it received a favorable ruling from the U.S. Internal Revenue Service to distribute Ablest's common stock to its shareholders. Ablest, which specializes in supplying clerical workers and workers for light industries, has 30 offices in the Midwestern and Southeastern United States. C.H. Heist provides cleaning, insulation, and maintenance services in the United States and Canada. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610
