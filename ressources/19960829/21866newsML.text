NEW YORK, Aug 29 (LPC) - CIBC Wood Gundy and Bank of Nova Scotia will syndicate a $180 million loan to fund in part a management buyout of Toronto Sun Publishing Corp, banking sources said. The deal is split between a $15 million 364-day operating facility and a $165 million seven-year term loan, a banker said. Only the term loan, which will be fully drawn at closing of the management buyout, will be syndicated, sources said. The buyout has been estimated to total $411 million, market sources said. Toronto Sun Publishing owns several Canadian newspapers and a majority interest in The Financial Post. Shareholders of the newspaper concern will vote on the buyout plan, which would pay $16 per share, on September 24, bankers said. --Beth Harmen, LPC Editorial, 212 489-5455
