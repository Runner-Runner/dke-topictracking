Time Telecommunications Sdn Bhd, a wholly-owned unit of Time Engineering Bhd, said on Thursday it has invested 60 million ringgit to set up a satellite station on Labuan isle, off Borneo island. Group chairman Shamsir Omar said the company has landed its first corporate and residential customers at Labuan, home to Malaysia's International Offshore Financial Centre. He told reporters at a news conference that Time Telekom has targeted about 50 percent of business from the Labuan financial park, which houses the 50 banks and several insurance and trust companies which form the offshore centre.
