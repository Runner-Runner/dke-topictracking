Taiwan's central bank on Thursday injected T$17.405 billion worth of funds into the banking system through open market operations, a central bank official said. The bank, through three finance companies, bought T$17.405 billion in approved securities instruments offered by financial institutions with yields at 5.500-5.625 percent, the official said. The institutions have the option of repurchasing the instruments four to 28 days from now, the official said. -- Taipei Newsroom (5080815)
