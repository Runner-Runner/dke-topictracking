Bugaboo Creek Steak House Inc said Thursday Chief Financial Officer Mark Peterson has resigned in light of the company's pending merger with Longhorn Steaks Inc. The merger is scheduled to be completed September 13. The company said Peterson will be joining Applebee's International Inc as vice president and controller in early September. Bugaboo Creek said Anne Huemme, chief financial officer of Longhorn, will act as CFO for the combined companies. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
