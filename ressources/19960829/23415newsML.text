The Bank of Portugal offered to inject 10 billion escudos of six-day funds into the money market in a variable rate securities repurchase tender. The central bank also offered to drain liquidity today with six-day certificates at a fixed rate of 6.8 percent. Its emergency lending rate for up to 100 billion escudos of one-day funds to resident banks was unchanged at 9.00 percent. Bids for the repo funds must be submitted by 0900 GMT and bids for the certificates by 1300 GMT. --LISBON BUREAU 3511-3538254
