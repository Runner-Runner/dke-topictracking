Halcyon/Alan B. Slifka Management Co LLC said on Thursday it has now acquired a total of 1.19 million shares or 7.74 percent of the common stock of Circo Craft Co Inc for accounts managed by it. About 510,000 of these shares were acquired through The Toronto Stock Exchange and the Montreal Stock Exchange since August 9, 1996 the date C-Mac Industries Inc's takeover bid for Circo, the firm said. Halcyon/Alan B. Slifka Management said it has bought the shares for investment purposes and may continue to buy and may sell the shares on the basis of market conditions.
