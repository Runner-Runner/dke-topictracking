The State (central) Bank of Pakistan cut interest rates on five-year foreign currency bearer certificates on Thursday. The bank said it had reduced the rate for dollar certificates to 9.0 percent a year from 9.25 percent. The rate for sterling certificates was cut to 9.75 percent from 10 percent, for marks to 7.75 percent from 8.0 percent and for yen to 4.50 percent from 5.0 percent. -- Karachi newsroom 9221-5685192
