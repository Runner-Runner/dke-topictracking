Singapore light distillate stocks fell to their lowest level in 20 months, Trade Development Board (TDB) data showed in the week ended August 28. Light distillate stocks fell by 168,000 barrels to 4.711 million barrels, the latest data showed.   This is the lowest level since at least December 1994, according to records immediately at hand. Light distillates include gasoline, reformate, naphtha but exclude gases. --Singapore Newsroom (+65-8703086)
