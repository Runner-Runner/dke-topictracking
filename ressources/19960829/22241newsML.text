USA Waste Services Inc and Sanifill Inc said on Thursday that their shareholders have approved their merger. The companies said they are discussing anti-trust regulatory issues with the Department of Justice and believe the merger will close this month. The companies say they expect combined annualized revenues of approximately $1.3 billion and total assets in excess of $2.0 billion. They said the merger USA Waste the third largest solid waste company in North America. USA Waste, based in Dallas, Texas, serves municipal, commercial, industrial and residential customers in 26 states and Canada. Sanifill, headquartered in Houston, handles the management and disposal of non-hazardous waste in 23 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, Mexico and Canada. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
