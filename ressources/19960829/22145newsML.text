Western Country Clubs Inc president Troy Lowrie said he is in talks with a group of individuals regarding the sale of a "substantial portion" of his shares in the company. Speaking in a press release, Lowrie said the proposed buyers are persons who have experience in managing and owning businesses similar to Western. The proposed sale is contingent on certain conditions including a $7,000,000 firm commitment from an underwriter. --New York Newsdesk 212 859 1610
