Sun Co said it raised the price it will pay for U.S. crude oil by 25 cents a barrel, effective today. The increase brings the new price for U.S. benchmark grade West Texas Intermediate to $20.50 a barrel, and West Texas Sour to $18.00 a barrel. Light Louisiana Sweet crude is now $20.25 a barrel, and Oklahoma Sweet is $20.50. -- New York Energy Desk, +1 212 859-1620
