Vitro Corp, a subsidiary of Tracor Inc, said on Thursday it was awarded a five-year contract from UNISYS Federal Systems Division of McLean, Va., to support the Defense Information Systems Agency DEIS II program. A statement from Tracor said the program will provide integrated services for the U.S. Dept. of Defense. Contracts were awarded to six vendors and the potential value to Vitro is $50 million, Tracor said.
