The Philadelphia Stock Exchange (PHLX) said on Thursday it would list options on Caraustar Industries Inc and Stein Mart Inc on September 3. The exchange said Caraustar will have initial expiration months of September, October, December and March and Stein Mart will have initial expirations of September, October, January and April. Opening strike prices will be 25, 30 and 35 for Caraustar and 20, 22-1/2 and 25 for Stein Mart, it said.   The PHLX said TFM Investment Group will be the specialist for both Caraustar and Stein Mart options. Caraustar makes recycled paperboard and converted paperboard products and Stein Mart is a discount retailer. --Chicago derivatives desk, (312) 408-8750 E-mail: derivatives@reuters.com
