Chicago Miniature Lamp Inc filed Thursday with the Securities and Exchange Commission for up to 3.7 million common shares. The Canton, Mass.-based company said it will sell 2.7 million shares, while one million will be offered by shareholders. After the offering, there will be 18,589,201 common shares outstanding. Proceeds from the offering will be used to repay certain debt, finance the purchase and installation of certain manufacturing equipment, and for general corporate purposes, including possible future acquisitions. The offering will be made through Smith Barney Inc, Raymond James & Associates Inc and McDonald & Co Securities Inc. The underwriters have an over-allotment option for up to 555,000 common shares.
