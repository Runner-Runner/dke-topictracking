The European Union on Thursday sold 9,622 tonnes of German intervention rye at a minimum price of 100.13 Ecus per tonne, France's grain office said. ONIC also confirmed earlier grain industry reports that the EU's grain panel sold 234,324 tonnes of German intervention barley at a minimum price of 105.07 Ecus per tonnes for export to non-EU states. --Paris newsroom +331 4221 5432
