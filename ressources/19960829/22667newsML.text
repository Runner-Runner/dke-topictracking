Praxair Inc said Tuesday it was giving its president greater responsibilities with an eye toward expanding its business in Asia. Company President Edgar Hotard will remain as president, but expand his duties to include overall development of the company's Asia strategy. He also will be responsible for its European operations. Praxair, an industrial gases company, currently operates in China, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Singapore and Thailand. The company said its capital investments in Asia are expected to exceed $600 million in the next five years.
