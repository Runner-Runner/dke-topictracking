Australian commercial paper issuance picked up on Thursday with a total of A$55 million worth of promissory notes sold at tender. Biscuit-maker Arnotts Ltd sold A$15 million of December two-dated p-notes and A$10 million of December 16 notes. Both parcels sold at 6.94 percent. Keystart Bonds issued A$30 million of late Novemeber-dated paper at three basis points over the mid-rate for November 27-dated bank bills. Dealers said issuance levels were expected to rise over the next week as a result of a short-term glut of maturing p-notes.
