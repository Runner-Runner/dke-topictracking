The Bangladesh taka edged up slightly to 42.2320/42.2420 to the dollar in interbank trading compared with the previous 42.2370/42.2470, dealers said. Dealers said the taka strengthened further on inflow of dollars through remittances and lack of demand from importers. The taka opening rates were at 42.2350/42.2450 against the dollar. Dealers said the interbank call money rates closed mostly steady between 4.0 percent and 4.75 percent. -- Dhaka Newsroom 880-2-506363
