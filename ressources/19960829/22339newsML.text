Uniflex Inc said Thursday its common will be split 3-for-2 in the form of a 50 percent stock dividend payable October 15 to shareholders of record on September 25. Currently, there are 2,994,000 shares outstanding, the company said. "We recently reported record sales and profits for the company's second quarter and six months ended July 31, 1996, and expect that the company will achieve another year of record results," Uniflex said. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
