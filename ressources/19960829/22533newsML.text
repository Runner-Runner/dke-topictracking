The Moroccan Bank al-Maghrib (central bank) withdrew 500 million dirhams from the money market for one week at its repurchase auction, a bank official said on Thursday. The bank said it received bids totalling 4.618 billion dirhams and allocated 2.000 billion dirhams at seven percent interest rate for one week. The amount allocated was less than 2.500 billion dirhams leaving the market this week, the source said. The bank satisfied 43.31 percent of demand for liquidity, it said. The next auction will be on September 4, 1996. ($1= 8.6 dirhams) -- Rabat newsroom (212-7) 726518
