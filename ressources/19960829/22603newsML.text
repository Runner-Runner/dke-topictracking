MasTec Inc filed Thursday with the Securities and Exchange Commission for up to 2.6 million common shares. Although the Miami-based company registered 2.6 million shares, it did not specifiy how many would be offered by the company and how many would be offered by stockholders. Net proceeds will be used to fund the company's growth strategy, including making acquisitions, funding working capital, capital expenditures and other general corporate purposes, MasTec said in the filing. The offering will be made through Bear Stearns and Co Inc and Jefferies and Co Inc.
