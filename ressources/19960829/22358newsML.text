Landec Corp said Thursday it will not to proceed with its previously announced secondary common stock offering. "Conditions in the securities markets have changed significantly since we originally filed our registration statement on July 3," said Gary Steele, president and chief executive officer. "Landec has sufficient working capital to fund our growth for the foreseeable future, so we believe it is in the best interest of the company and our shareholders to withdraw the public offering," he added.   Landec designs, develops, manufactures and sells polymer products for industrial, medical and agricultural applications. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1700.
