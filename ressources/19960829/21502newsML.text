Preliminary figures for Brazil's commercial and financial foreign exchange contracts showed a $90.3 million deficit on August 28 in the commercial forex market according to Central Bank data supplied to banks. A banker with access to the figures said the central bank issued the following breakdown. DOLLAR FLOWS ON AUGUST 28 Exports: $231.3 million Imports: $191.8 million Financial buy: $179.1 million Financial sell: $308.9 million Deficit: $90.3 million AUGUST DOLLAR FLOWS UP TO DATE Exports: $4.219 billion Imports: $3.347 billion Financial buy: $4.233 billion Financial sell: $3.692 billion Net surplus: $1.413 billion Note: the figures were rounded off. -- Alexandre Caverni, Sao Paulo newsroom, 55-11-2324411
