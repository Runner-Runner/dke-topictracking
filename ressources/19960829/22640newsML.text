Chicago Board of Trade's Project A overnight agricultural contracts trading volume totaled 546 contracts, according to the CBOT. There were 54 wheat contracts, 208 corn, 246 soybean, 35 soymeal, nil soyoil, 11 oats and 13 rough rice futures contracts traded, along with 1 soybean call option and 6 soybean puts. The overnight trend for corn was 1/2 to 3/4 cent per bushel lower, soybeans were up 1 to down 2-1/4 cents, wheat down 2-1/4 cents, soymeal up 80 cents to $2.80 per ton, oats steady to dwon two cents, and rough rice steady.
