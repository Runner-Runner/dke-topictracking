The Egyptian state said on Thursday it would sell 450,000 shares, or 90 percent, of contracting firm Nasr Public Utilities and Installation to one or more strategic investors. The parent company Holding National for Construction and Development said the deadline for receiving bids was noon local time (0900 gmt) on September 21. Bids can be submitted for at least of 50,000 shares (corrects from 5,000 shares) or 10 percent stake of the total shares. $1 = 3.4 pounds. -- Esmat Salahuddin, Cairo Newsroom +202 578 3290/1
