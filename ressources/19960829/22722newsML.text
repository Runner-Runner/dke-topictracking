Germany's 1996 rapeseed harvest was estimated at two million tonnes compared with 3.1 million tonnes in 1995, the Bonn farm ministry said on Thursday. The ministry cited winter damage to the crop. Yields were put at 2.27 tonnes per hectare, versus 3.19 tonnes in 1995. The ministry stressed the figure was preliminary. --Bonn newsroom, 49 228 26097150
