Malaysia's tin price fell three cents to 15.03 ringgit ($6.03) a kg despite a higher overnight close on the London Metal Exchange (LME), traders said. "There were higher offerings today and the market did not take its cue from the LME," said a dealer. The LME rose $5 on Wednesday. Turnover rose marginally to 114 tonnes from 113 on Tuesday. Bids totalled 86 tonnes against offers of 147. The local premium eased to $30 from $45. Buyers were Europeans and locals. -- Barani Krishnaan (603-230-7509) ($1=2.4940 ringgit)
