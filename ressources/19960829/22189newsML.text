U.S. M-2 money supply was unchanged in the August 19 week at $3,764.6 billion, the Federal Reserve said. The broader M-3 measure rose $400 million to $4,759.0 billion. M-1 fell $3.7 billion to $1,096.7 billion, the Fed said. The Fed said the four-week moving average of M-2 was $3,762.3 billion versus $3,759.4 billion in the previous week. -- N.A.
