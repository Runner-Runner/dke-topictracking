Indonesia's central bank, Bank Indonesia, supplied some 244 billion rupiah temporarily on the money market early on Thursday, money traders said. Some 842 billion rupiah of central bank certificates (SBIs) were due today, compared with some 148 billion rupiah of maturing short-term papers (SBPUs) and 450 bilion rupiah of weekly SBIs to be settled today. -- Jakarta newsroom +6221 384-6364.
