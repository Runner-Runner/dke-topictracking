MedPartners/Mullikin Inc said Thursday the U.S. Federal Trade Commission terminated the waiting period under the Hart Scott Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act regarding the proposed merger with Caremark International Inc. This decision was the final regulatory clearance necessary for the completion of the proposed $2.5 billion merger announced on May 14. The merger of the nation's two largest physician practice management firms is expected to close following a favorable vote by shareholders on September 5. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610
