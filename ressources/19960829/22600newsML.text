The Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp (Freddie Mac or FHLMC) said it issued $200 million of callable pass-through securities. Callable pass-through series C23, underwritten by DLJ Securities Inc, has a coupon of 8.0 percent and is backed by Freddie Mac strip 178, FHLMC said. The issue priced August 20 and has a settlement date of September 27, the agency said. --N.A. Treasury Desk, 212-859-1660
