U.S. Xpress Enterprises Inc said Thursday Larry Bentley will resign as its director, executive vice president and chief financial officer, effective September 13. The company declined to give a reason for Bentley's resignation. It said Donald Rutledge, formerly general manager of U.S. Xpress Inc, the company's largest subsidiary, will serve as interim chief financial officer. The company said a nationwide search is underway to select Bentley's successor. -- New York Newsdesk 212-859-1610.
