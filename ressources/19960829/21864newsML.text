The prospectus covering Houston Exploration Co's planned public offering is being amended to include the company's June quarter results, Brooklyn Union Gas Co spokesman Robert Loftus said. The utility now expects its subsidiary's initial public offering sometime this fall, Loftus added. Shortly after Houston Exploration registered 5.7 million shares May 24, company officials said they expected the offering in July. Recently, the underwriters led by Goldman Sachs & Co have been saying mid-September. -- Jim Brumm 212-859-1710.
