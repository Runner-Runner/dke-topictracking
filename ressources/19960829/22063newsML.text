The share of Gujarat Ambuja Cement rose 19.25 rupees or 6.23 percent, to 328.25 on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) on Thursday following good results announced by the firm, brokers said.   The firm said on Tuesday that its net profit jumped 47 percent to 1.48 billion rupees in year to June 30, 1996 from 1.0 billion a year ago, while net sales grew to 7.3 billion rupees from 4.3 billion. The BSE was closed on Wednesday for a holiday. -- Bombay newsroom +91-22-265 9000
